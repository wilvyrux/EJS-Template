Spine Institute of San Diego 2

Our goal at the Spine Institute of San Diego is to maximize function through muscle preservation. We believe in maximizing non-operative treatment. If surgery Is required, we employ the latest endoscopic and minimally invasive techniques to preserve muscle function, as well as leading edge surgical navigation techniques to maximize safety. We also specialize in disc replacement surgeries.
